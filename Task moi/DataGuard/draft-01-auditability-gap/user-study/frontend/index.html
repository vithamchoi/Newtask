DataGuard Audit Study

Welcome. In this study you will compare the short Google Play Data Safety summary for an Android app with the same app's longer privacy policy and judge whether the summary is consistent.

This expert-feedback version takes about 15–25 minutes and uses a small set of sample audit tasks. Your responses are anonymous. Please read the consent below before starting.

Informed consent (summary)

  • Adult participants only (18+).
  • Your responses are stored under a random token; no personally identifying information is collected.
  • You may stop at any time without penalty.
  • De-identified per-trial data may be released alongside the paper.
  • This deployment is for formative expert feedback on the study materials and interface.
