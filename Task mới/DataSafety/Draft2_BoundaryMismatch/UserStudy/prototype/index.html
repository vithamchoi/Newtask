Boundary-Layer Explainer prototype

This is the working prototype that accompanies the paper Where the Policy Ends and the User Begins: A Boundary-Case Mismatch in App-Store Privacy Labels.

1) Live participant flow — full study

The participant arrives via Prolific (or Microworkers) with a unique ?pid=PROLIFIC_ID. The single-page-app study.html runs consent → pre-task → 4 randomised app vignettes → attention check → post-task → debrief in one continuous flow. Condition (current vs BLE) is assigned by hashing the pid, and the 4-app subset is sampled by stratification from a 12-app pool.

2) Condition-only previews

For design review and the think-aloud arm. No consent flow, no instrument — just the rendered label so the moderator can walk participants through it.

Preview mode (no pid)

Without a pid, the prototype shows the full pool so moderators can inspect any single app. Useful for the think-aloud arm and for design review.
