# Recruitment texts

## Prolific posting — vignette experiment

**Title:** Understanding app privacy labels — a short study

**Short description (Prolific):**
We are studying how people read the privacy information that app
stores show before you install a new app. In this study you will look
at the privacy section of four fictional apps and answer a few short
questions about each. The study should take 15--20 minutes and pays
£3 (about USD $3).

**Eligibility:**
- Current Android user
- 18 years or older
- Fluent in English
- Prolific approval rating $\geq 95\%$
- $\geq 50$ prior submissions on Prolific

**Estimated time:** 15 minutes
**Reward:** USD $3

## Prolific posting — think-aloud sessions

**Title:** Talk-through study about app privacy labels (Zoom session)

**Short description:**
We are running a small, in-depth study about how people read the
privacy section of an app store. You will join a 30--45 minute Zoom
session with a researcher and walk us through four privacy labels for
fictional apps while sharing your screen. The session pays a USD~$25
e-gift card. We will record the audio of the call (no video) for
research analysis.

**Eligibility:**
- Current Android user
- 18 years or older
- Comfortable speaking aloud in English while reading on a screen
- Have a quiet space with a webcam and microphone

**Estimated time:** 45 minutes (including 5 min buffer)
**Reward:** USD $25 e-gift card

## Screening
- A 60-second pre-screener asks 3 questions and disqualifies any
  participant who reports not currently using Android, or who reports
  not being comfortable speaking aloud while reading on a screen.
- Participants in the vignette arm are excluded from the think-aloud
  arm and vice versa (cross-arm contamination control).
