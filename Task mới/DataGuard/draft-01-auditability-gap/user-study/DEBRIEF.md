# Post-Study Debrief

Thank you for completing the DataGuard audit study.

## What were we studying?

You compared three interfaces for auditing Android app *Data Safety* labels
against the corresponding privacy policies:

- **C0 — raw review**: what a regulator or motivated user sees today
  (the structured Data Safety panel and the full privacy-policy text).
- **C1 — structured side-by-side**: the same content, reorganised so the
  relevant policy passages are visible without scrolling.
- **C2 — evidence-grounded AI**: C1 plus highlighted candidate evidence and
  an AI-suggested judgment with an uncertainty flag.

We are measuring whether C1 and C2 improve the accuracy and confidence
calibration of the audit verdict, and whether C2 lowers perceived workload
without increasing over-reliance on the AI.

## What happens with your data?

- Your responses are stored under a random session token only; we cannot
  trace them back to you.
- We will analyse aggregated data only.
- De-identified per-trial data and the analysis scripts will be released
  alongside the manuscript.

## Why is this important?

Mobile-app privacy disclosures are easy to read but hard to verify against
the underlying policy. When the disclosure is wrong or incomplete, end users
and regulators have no easy way to find out. Our previous work documents the
*auditability gap*; this study tests whether evidence-grounded interfaces
help close it.
